Privacy Policy

Effective 28 March 2026

Elusive Analytics ("we", "us") is operated by Elusive Management. This policy explains what data the dashboard collects, how it is stored, and your rights.

1. What we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your Google account email address, display name, and profile picture.
  • An OAuth access token and refresh token that grant read-only access to your YouTube channel and YouTube Analytics data.

We use those tokens to retrieve:

  • Channel statistics (subscribers, views, watch time).
  • Video metadata (titles, thumbnails, publish dates, view counts, likes, comments).
  • Analytics data (daily views, audience demographics, traffic sources, retention).

We do not access your Gmail, Google Drive, contacts, or any data outside of YouTube.

When you connect your Instagram account, we receive:

  • An OAuth access token via Instagram Login that grants read-only access to your Instagram Business or Creator account.
  • Your Instagram username, profile picture, and follower count.

We use that token to retrieve:

  • Follower count and media count.
  • Account insights (reach over the last 30 days).
  • Recent post metrics (likes, comments) to calculate engagement rate.

We do not access your Instagram direct messages, post to your account, or access any data outside of your Instagram Business or Creator account.

2. How tokens are stored

Your Google and Instagram OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using Fernet symmetric encryption (AES-128-CBC with HMAC-SHA256) before they are saved to the database. The encryption key is held as a server-side environment variable and is never exposed to the browser or included in logs.

3. How data is used

Your YouTube and Instagram data is used solely to populate the Elusive Analytics dashboard so that you and your management team can track channel performance, identify trends, and prepare for brand partnerships. We never sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data retention

We retain your analytics snapshots (daily stats, video metrics) for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, all stored data — including tokens, stats, and profile information — will be permanently removed within 30 days.

5. Third-party services

  • Google OAuth / YouTube Data API — used to authenticate you and fetch YouTube channel data. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Instagram API — used to connect your Instagram account via Instagram Login and fetch account insights. Subject to Instagram's Privacy Policy.
  • Railway — cloud hosting provider where the application and database run. Subject to Railway's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used on this dashboard.

6. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. It contains only a session identifier — no personal data. No third-party cookies or tracking pixels are present.

7. Your rights

You can:

  • Revoke the dashboard's access to your YouTube data at any time via your Google account permissions.
  • Disconnect Instagram at any time via Instagram Settings > Apps and Websites. This triggers automatic removal of your Instagram token from our database.
  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
